01 — Profile

I lead at the intersection of enterprise scale and scientific rigour.

At EY GDS, I serve as Associate Director for AI & Advanced Analytics and as a location leader in Sri Lanka — owning the technical-architecture portfolio, shaping annual roadmaps, and converting the firm's value proposition in front of clients. My job is to make ambitious AI both deliverable and defensible.

Before EY, I spent nearly nine years at Axiata growing from Principal Architect to Senior Principal Architect for Data Science & Engineering — standing up an end-to-end AI Factory: the model lifecycle, MLOps automation, deployment platform, and an ethical-AI practice that answered to auditors, regulators, and governance teams alike.

My foundation is two decades inside telecom — real-time charging, converged CRM, and big-data platforms operating at national scale. Alongside it runs a research thread I never set down: computational genomics and bioinformatics, from pangenome graphs to containerized workflow tooling. Few AI leaders ship production platforms and publish peer-reviewed science. I do both, and each sharpens the other.
